About Us

We at Jeffers & Son's are a local small bath supplies company that strives to meet the needs of you, the customer. Started in 1908, our soap recipes have been passed down three generations and refined to meet the needs today. Our mission is to provide not only the best cleaning product, but one that leaves you feeling refreshed and clean.
